大图标使用


icon-lg

icon-2x

icon-3x

icon-4x

icon-5x

为了增加图标大小相对于它们的容器，使用icon-lg （增大33 ％ ） ， icon-lg (33% increase), icon-2x, icon-3x, icon-4x, or icon-5x classes. 

<i class="icon icon-camera-retro icon-lg"></i> icon-lg
<i class="icon icon-camera-retro icon-2x"></i> icon-2x
<i class="icon icon-camera-retro icon-3x"></i> icon-3x
<i class="icon icon-camera-retro icon-4x"></i> icon-4x
<i class="icon icon-camera-retro icon-5x"></i> icon-5x
If your icons are getting chopped off on top and bottom, make sure you have sufficient line-height.
